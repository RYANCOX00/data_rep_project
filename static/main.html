Welcome to Treasury Manager.

The business model for this company, "ABC Currency Solutions" is to provide a currency exchange solution for acquiring banks. Each bank will have their own customers. These customers are retail outlets also known as merchants.

Acquiring banks will accept mutli-currency transactions on behalf of merchants through their point of sale terminals.

ABC Currency Solutions mananges the full currency exchange and calculates the commission to be split between the acquirering bank and ABC Currency Solutions.

On this page you will be able to access all the tables from the Transaction Convertion database. These tables include:

  • All acquirers
  • All merchants
  • Current exchange rates.
  • All transactions
  • Commission splits.

The exchange rates are available under the "View Latest Rates".
The transaction and commission split reports are available under "Finance Reports".
Acquirer and Merchant tables and be viewed and updated under "Acqurier and Merchant Manager".
